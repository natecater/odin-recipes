Smoothie Recipe

This is my own smoothie recipe.

  1. Add a banana
  2. Add frozen fruit (berry medley or tropical mix)
  3. Add about a cup of almond milk
  4. Add supplemental powders: protein, collagen, creatine
  5. Add hemp seeds and/or chia seeds, or something similar
  6. Add peanut or almond butter
  7. (optional) Add half a cup of oatmeal for extra calories #gainszn
  8. Add some extra water for whatever consistency you'd like
  9. Blend it up
  10. Drink it
